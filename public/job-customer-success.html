Job Overview

We're seeking a passionate Customer Success Manager to join our growing team and help drive the success of our PEA (Product Experience Analytics) community. In this role, you'll be the primary advocate for our users, ensuring they achieve maximum value from our platform while building lasting relationships that drive retention and growth.

Why This Role Matters

As a Customer Success Manager at CDOT Database, you'll directly impact thousands of businesses worldwide by helping them leverage our platform to make data-driven decisions, optimize their product experiences, and drive growth. You'll be at the forefront of customer advocacy, working with cross-functional teams to ensure our users not only succeed but thrive.

Key Responsibilities

Customer Relationship Management
Build and maintain strong relationships with key customers, serving as their primary point of contact and trusted advisor.
Success Planning & Execution
Develop and execute customer success plans to drive adoption, retention, and expansion of our PEA platform.
Training & Onboarding
Lead customer onboarding processes and provide ongoing training to ensure users maximize platform value.
Support & Troubleshooting
Provide expert-level support, resolving complex customer issues and coordinating with technical teams when needed.
Data Analysis & Insights
Analyze customer usage data to identify trends, risks, and opportunities for improvement and growth.
Expansion & Upselling
Identify opportunities for account expansion and work with sales teams to drive revenue growth.
Feedback & Advocacy
Gather customer feedback and advocate for product improvements to enhance user experience.
Presentations & Demos
Conduct product demonstrations, webinars, and training sessions for customers and prospects.

Required Qualifications

Experience: 3+ years in customer success, account management, or related role in SaaS
Education: Bachelor's degree in Business, Marketing, Communications, or related field
Communication: Excellent written and verbal communication skills
Problem Solving: Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities
Relationship Building: Proven ability to build and maintain strong customer relationships
Technical Aptitude: Comfortable with SaaS platforms, analytics tools, and CRM systems
Project Management: Experience managing multiple accounts and projects simultaneously
Results-Driven: Track record of meeting or exceeding customer success KPIs

Preferred Qualifications

Industry Experience: Background in e-commerce, affiliate marketing, or analytics platforms
Certifications: Customer Success Manager (CSM) or similar professional certifications
Data Analysis: Experience with data visualization tools and customer analytics
Technical Skills: Familiarity with APIs, integrations, and technical product features
Languages: Multilingual capabilities for global customer base
Leadership: Experience mentoring junior team members or leading initiatives
