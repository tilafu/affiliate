What is the PEA Program?

The Product Engagement Associate (PEA) Program is a structured way to earn through digital work while supporting real eCommerce businesses. PEAs help store owners boost product visibility and trust across top platforms like Amazon, eBay, and Shopify by participating in a verified, performance-driven system.

How PEAs Boost Product Visibility

At the core of the program is a method called sales prototyping. PEAs make refundable purchases of selected products through live online stores. These transactions are tracked by the platform, helping improve search rankings and build social proof signals like "purchased recently" or "bought by thousands."

Every PEA:

  • Receives a full refund for each approved product
  • Earns a commission per engagement
  • Has all transactions securely processed through CDOT

This creates momentum for the product and trust for future buyers, while rewarding PEAs for their role in the process.

Earning Model

PEAs can engage with products daily, within a limit set by their membership level. This system ensures fairness across the platform.

For each product engagement:

  • A commission is earned
  • A full refund is issued
  • A base salary is also paid by CDOT

This combination creates a stable, scalable income model that works for both part-time users and those more committed.

Flexible and Reliable Work

The PEA Program is not gig work or basic task-hunting. It's a structured platform that supports real businesses and rewards consistency. Whether you're a student, business owner, or full-time professional, the flexible format allows you to earn without disrupting your schedule.
