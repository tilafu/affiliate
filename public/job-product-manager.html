Job Overview

We're seeking a dynamic Product Manager to lead product strategy and development for our PEA (Product Engagement Associate) platform. You'll work with cross-functional teams to deliver innovative features that empower our global network of 50,000+ PEAs and drive business growth.

As the Product Manager for our PEA Platform, you'll be responsible for defining the product roadmap, gathering user feedback, analyzing market trends, and working closely with engineering and design teams to deliver exceptional user experiences. You'll play a crucial role in scaling our platform to serve millions of users worldwide.

Key Responsibilities

  • Define and execute product strategy for the PEA platform
  • Develop comprehensive product roadmaps aligned with business objectives
  • Conduct user research and gather feedback from our global PEA community
  • Analyze product metrics and user behavior to drive data-driven decisions
  • Collaborate with engineering teams to prioritize features and manage product backlog
  • Work with design teams to create intuitive user experiences
  • Coordinate product launches and go-to-market strategies
  • Monitor competitive landscape and identify market opportunities
  • Communicate product vision and updates to stakeholders across the organization

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Business, Engineering, Computer Science, or related field
  • 4+ years of product management experience, preferably in tech or e-commerce
  • Strong analytical skills with experience in data analysis and metrics
  • Experience with Agile development methodologies
  • Proven track record of launching successful products
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills
  • Strong project management and organizational abilities
  • Experience with user research and usability testing
  • Ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams

Preferred Qualifications

  • MBA or advanced degree in relevant field
  • Experience in e-commerce, marketplace, or affiliate marketing platforms
  • Knowledge of user experience (UX) design principles
  • Experience with A/B testing and experimentation frameworks
  • Familiarity with product analytics tools (Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.)
  • Understanding of global markets and localization challenges
  • Experience managing products with large user bases (100k+ users)
