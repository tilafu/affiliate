Job Overview

We're looking for a creative and data-driven Digital Marketing Specialist to drive growth through digital marketing campaigns, content creation, and community building for our global PEA network. You'll play a key role in expanding our reach across 60+ countries.

As our Digital Marketing Specialist, you'll be responsible for developing and executing comprehensive digital marketing strategies that attract new PEAs to our platform, engage our existing community, and build brand awareness in the e-commerce and affiliate marketing space.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and execute comprehensive digital marketing campaigns across multiple channels
  • Create engaging content for social media, blog posts, email campaigns, and video content
  • Manage and optimize SEO/SEM campaigns to increase organic and paid traffic
  • Build and nurture our global PEA community through social media engagement
  • Analyze marketing performance metrics and optimize campaigns for better ROI
  • Collaborate with product and sales teams on go-to-market strategies
  • Manage influencer partnerships and affiliate marketing relationships
  • Conduct market research and competitive analysis
  • Coordinate with international teams for localized marketing initiatives

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, or related field
  • 3+ years of experience in digital marketing or related roles
  • Strong understanding of SEO, SEM, and social media marketing
  • Experience with content creation and copywriting
  • Proficiency in marketing analytics tools (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Experience with email marketing platforms and automation
  • Strong project management and organizational skills
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Creative mindset with attention to detail

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience in e-commerce, affiliate marketing, or marketplace platforms
  • Knowledge of conversion rate optimization (CRO) techniques
  • Experience with marketing automation and CRM systems
  • Video editing and graphic design skills
  • Familiarity with paid advertising platforms (Facebook Ads, Google Ads)
  • Understanding of international markets and cultural differences
  • Experience with community building and engagement strategies
