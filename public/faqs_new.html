Getting Started

Taking Membership level 1 as an example, the minimum account balance for the first 40 optimization tasks is $50.

For resetting the product optimization task counter at Membership level 1, a minimum of $100 is required.

Once 40 product optimization tasks are completed at Membership level 1, users must apply to reset the account and complete the day's work before applying for full withdrawal.

Cash Withdrawals

Users must complete all product optimization tasks before applying for cash withdrawal.

If you choose to give up or exit during the product optimization task, you cannot apply for cancellation or refund.

Full withdrawal is required upon completion of all tasks.

If your withdrawal amount exceeds the limit, you must upgrade to a membership level that can accommodate the withdrawal.

Upgrading between membership levels requires a $5000 deposit, which will be refunded when you downgrade.

Financial Security

All funds are safely held in user accounts and can be withdrawn in full upon completion of all product optimization tasks.

To avoid economic losses, all data is processed by the system without manual processing.

The platform bears full responsibility for any economic losses caused by system incidents.

Do not disclose your login password and security code to others. The platform will not be responsible for losses caused by sharing credentials.

Avoid using birthdays, ID numbers, or mobile phone numbers as security codes or login passwords.

If you forget your login password or security code, contact our online customer service to reset it.

Platform Operations

Platform income is divided into ordinary income and enhanced income. All users are generally limited to a maximum of 3 merge orders.

Membership level 1 receives 0.5% profit from each normal product optimization.

Membership level 2 receives 1.0% profit from each portfolio application.

After each product optimization is completed, funds and profits are returned to the user account.

A portfolio application consists of 1 to 3 product orders. Users don't necessarily get 3 combined products.

For each product order in the merged order, users receive 5 times the normal product optimization commission.

Upon receipt of merged orders, all funds are returned to your account upon completion.

Merged orders cannot be canceled or skipped once assigned to a user account.

Users can start data optimization during daily business hours from 10:00 to 23:00.

Withdrawal processing time is from 10:30 to 22:30 daily.

For further explanation, please contact our online customer service!

Deposits & Referrals

The deposit amount is chosen by the user. We recommend making deposits based on your financial ability.

When receiving portfolio applications requiring deposits, make payments based on the difference amount shown in your account.

Always obtain deposit details from online customer service and confirm before making payments.

The platform is not responsible for incorrect deposits made without confirmation.

Advanced users and Membership Level 3+ can use invitation codes to invite other users.

Accounts must complete all application optimizations before inviting others.

Referrers receive an additional 20% of the total profit from referred users' activities.
