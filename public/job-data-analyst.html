Job Overview

We're seeking a Senior Data Analyst to analyze e-commerce data trends and provide insights to optimize our platform performance and user experience. You'll work with large datasets from our global PEA network to drive data-driven decisions across the organization.

As our Senior Data Analyst, you'll be responsible for extracting insights from complex datasets, building dashboards and reports, and working closely with product, engineering, and business teams to improve platform performance and user engagement across our 60+ country network.

Key Responsibilities

  • Analyze large datasets to identify trends, patterns, and insights from PEA platform usage
  • Build and maintain automated dashboards and reports using Tableau and other visualization tools
  • Conduct statistical analysis to measure platform performance and user engagement
  • Collaborate with product teams to define KPIs and success metrics
  • Perform A/B testing analysis and provide recommendations for feature improvements
  • Create data models and forecasting models for business planning
  • Work with engineering teams to ensure data quality and integrity
  • Present findings and recommendations to stakeholders and leadership
  • Mentor junior analysts and support data-driven culture across the organization

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or related field
  • 4+ years of experience in data analysis or business intelligence roles
  • Expert-level proficiency in SQL and database management
  • Strong experience with Python for data analysis and statistical modeling
  • Proficiency in data visualization tools like Tableau, Power BI, or similar
  • Experience with statistical analysis and hypothesis testing
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
  • Excellent communication skills with ability to present complex findings clearly
  • Experience working with large datasets and data warehouses

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience in e-commerce, marketplace, or affiliate marketing analytics
  • Knowledge of machine learning techniques and predictive modeling
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure)
  • Familiarity with big data tools (Spark, Hadoop, etc.)
  • Experience with A/B testing frameworks and experimentation
  • Knowledge of web analytics tools (Google Analytics, Mixpanel)
  • Understanding of financial metrics and business KPIs

Data Tools & Technologies We Use
